The Soul Behind InkVeda

Tanvi Prajapat

"Art is not just what I do; it's how I breathe. Every mandala is a fragment of my soul, a pattern that whispered to me in silence."

Based in India, Tanvi is a self-taught artist specializing in the intricate worlds of Mandalas, Zentangle, and meditative doodles. Her journey with InkVeda began as a personal practice of mindfulness, which eventually transformed into a professional pursuit of capturing complex symmetries and patterns.

Her work is characterized by precision, patience, and a deep respect for the meditative process of creation. Each piece is handcrafted, often taking weeks of meticulous line work to complete.

Style

Medium
